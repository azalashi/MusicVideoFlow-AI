# Music Video Production Workflow

## Overview

This document describes the current end-to-end workflow used to produce music videos.

The purpose of this document is to visualize the current creative process before introducing AI automation.

It represents the manual workflow currently used during production and serves as the foundation for future system design.

---

## Phase 0 - Music Production

Create the music before starting video production.

Typical activities include:

- Song composition
- Arrangement
- Lyrics writing
- Mixing
- Mastering

At this stage, the overall story, emotions, and atmosphere of the music are usually already decided.

---

## Phase 1 - Concept Design

Define the overall concept of the music video.

Typical activities include:

- Determine the world setting
- Decide the visual style
- Define the emotional direction
- Decide the overall theme

Examples:

- School
- Romance
- Gacha Game
- Fantasy
- Sci-Fi

This phase determines the direction of the entire production.

---

## Phase 2 - Character Design

Design the main character of the music video.

Typical activities include:

- Choose the main character
- Decide clothing
- Define hairstyle
- Decide colors
- Design unique characteristics
- Create the main reference illustration

The main illustration becomes the reference for all future assets.

---

## Phase 3 - Story Analysis

Analyze the lyrics and existing story.

Typical activities include:

- Read the lyrics
- Understand the emotional flow
- Identify important scenes
- Determine visual storytelling

The goal is to translate the music into visual storytelling.

---

## Phase 4 - Scene Planning

Break the story into individual scenes.

Typical activities include:

- Divide the MV into scenes
- Decide scene transitions
- Determine scene objectives
- Estimate scene duration

Video production is managed by scenes rather than musical sections.

---

## Phase 5 - Asset Planning

Determine all assets required for each scene.

Typical assets include:

- Character illustrations
- Facial expressions
- Character poses
- Backgrounds
- Props
- UI elements
- Effects

This is one of the most important planning phases because missing assets often interrupt later production.

---

## Phase 6 - Asset Creation

Create all planned assets.

Typical activities include:

- Generate illustrations
- Generate character variations
- Create backgrounds
- Create props
- Review generated results
- Retry generation when necessary
- Select final assets

Multiple iterations are often required before obtaining usable assets.

---

## Phase 7 - Video Editing

Produce the final music video.

Typical activities include:

- Arrange assets
- Animate characters
- Add lyrics
- Add transitions
- Add visual effects
- Camera movement
- Timing adjustment
- Final rendering

This phase combines all production assets into the final video.

---

## Phase 8 - Publishing

Publish the completed music video.

Typical activities include:

- Upload to YouTube
- Publish to social media
- Create thumbnails
- Write descriptions
- Share announcements

Publishing marks the completion of the production workflow.
